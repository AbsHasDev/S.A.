The Shogun Assassin

Descendent of the Horsemen of The Hill, distant relative of fabled heroes such as Genghis Khan and The Legendary Afro-Samurai.

Born on a full moon, it is rumoured the Shogun cut his own ambilicle cord. He did this using a sharpened toe nail he grew and nurtured in the womb. The Shogun has been said to be a masterful horserider, the likes of which have never been seen since the Dothraki were cancelled on HBO.
